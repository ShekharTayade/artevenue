WE ARE BACK!!

We have started the production after the complete lockdown restrictions have been lifted. We are now taking orders online and in-store and will be able to ship our artworks to all locations, except in areas identified by government as red zones. Our deliveries to red zones will happen as soon as it becomes possible.

Safety Measures: We are following the government directives and guidelines for our staff and factory workers. We are mainting the social distancing through distributing our staff and workers in shifts. We are disinfecting all our materials, factories, stores and offices, and taking all the safety measures.

So go ahead, use the time at home to decorate the walls.

What about orders placed before lockdown? You need not worry about the orders already placed with us. Our production has began and artworks are being shipped now. We will intimate each customer as the artworks get shipped.

Let's support the lockdown, maintain social distancing and beat the virus.
