Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to our support center. You will find here the frequently asked questions and their anwers. If none of these answer your question then please feel free to get in touch with us here.

Artwork Details

What type of artworks do you sell?

We currently sell licensed art prints of the paintings and photography art. Everytime we sell an art print, we pay license fee to the artist/publishing house

What about original fine art?

Our platform (ArteVenue.com) is getting ready to sell original paintings online. We do have stock of original paintings in our stores in Bangalore.

What do you print art on?

For every art print that we sell, we have an option choose print surface - paper or canvas print.

Can I customize the size of art print?

Yes. Ours is the only website that offers complete customization.

You can choose size inch-by-inch between the minimum and maximum print size allowed by artist. The typical print size you can chose is between 8 inch and 60 inch.

You can choose from various frame options, choose the mount size and color.

Our website shows you exactly how your artwork looks like after you customize it.

You can also use a unique "Wall Color and Size Tool" to select the size, change the wall color and see approximately how the artwork will look like in a room view.

What is the quality of materials used?
Frame Made of Polystyrene, which is light weight, strong and durable. It has classy finish.
Paper NovaJet Matte Coated Premium Paper 230 (MCP 230). Bright white, smooth finish matte, top-coated with an ink-receptive layer. Ideal for printing fine art reproduction.
Canvas NovaJet Artistic Matte Canvas 410 (AMC 410). Water-resistant canvas, top-coated with an ink-receptive layer. Ideal for printing fine art reproductions.
Glass Clear Acrylic: Added protection, durability and clear visibility. Please note that canvas art prints are not covered with glass or acrylic.
How accurate is image colors shown and what I will get? and what about the size accuracy?

We strive for a high degree of image accuracy and verify the sizes of all images on our Website. Please note that on different devices, laptops and compter screens the color shades of images are seen with small variations.

Please be assured that you will only get the best quality that it can be becuase all our artworks are licensed and hence we use only genuine images for printing and only print upto the size that is possible keeping the best quality.

With ragards to the size, there may be small variations (up to 2" for larger sizes) in the finished products due to aspect ratio of an image, printer specifications etc.

Ordering

How do I place an order?

Add the product(s) you wish to order in your shopping cart by clicking on the "Add to Cart" button located next to the product image. When you're ready to complete your order, click "Continue Checkout" from within your cart and follow the instructions.

Does the image on your site accurately represent what I will be sent?

We strive for a high degree of image accuracy. You can expect a 95-100% accuracy to the image viewed on our site.

How accurate are the item dimensions listed on your web pages?

ArteVenue.com checks the dimensions of all images on our site, but due to industry standards sizes can vary slightly by a few mm.

What are the resolution requirements for digital upload for custom framing orders?

Our minimum resolution for digital uploads is 150 DPI. Since resolution reduces as you enlarge a photo, the higher the resolution of your file, the larger your print can be. If you'd like to print a larger photo than what is suggested, you'll need to upload a higher-resolution image of your photo to have a larger print to a maximum size of 40x60 inch.

What kind of image can I upload to be printed?

You can upload a .jpg, or .png, under 30MB and we'll print and frame it for you!

Payments

What type of payments do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, Netbanking, UPI, and Wallet payments.

What will happen if the payment gets deducted and I get an error or I don't receive the order confirmation?

There is nothing to worry. If the payment is deducted then the order will get processed. Sometimes there is lag between banks and payment gateway and our system or other system issues. But we do reconcile the data and the order will get processed.

Shipping & Delivery

What type of packaging do you use?

With frame: Your artwork has following packaging - protective covering over frame corners, shrink wrap over frame, additional hard board covering over frame edges, bubble/ foam wrap, outer hard board covering, outer shrink wrap.
Without frame: Your artwork is rolled with butter paper to protect it against dust and then inserted in a tube to keep from any kind of damage during transit.

In our years of shipping experience, there have been minimal damages during transit.

When will my artwork arrive?

We ship your artwork by courier in 1-3 days. It usually takes about 3-10 days depending on the delivery point. We are based in Bangalore. It ususally takes between 3-5 days to deliver in all metro and major cities.

How do I track my order?

All the orders are shipped through courier. You will receive a tracking number through which you can track your order.

You can also track your order online on our website at https:/www.artevenue.com/my-orders (if you have logged)
https:/www.artevenue.com/find-orders (if you have not logged)

How much will shipping cost?

Shipping anywhere in India is free.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently we ship within India for free. For internatioal shipping please get in touch with us and we can help with international shipping.

Returns and Cancellation

What is your return & cancellation policy? Order Cancellation:

Our artwork is customized as per your order. The production process starts as soon as you place the order. We are unable to cancel your order after the payment is made and you receive the confirmation.
However, we are happy to cancel the order for you before our production process begins, which is typically within an hour of you placing an order. In case you wish to cancel please call/write back to us as soon as possible, and we can let you know if the order can be cancelled.
If the order is cancelled, we will ask you for your bank details and it will take up to 7 business days to process the refund.

Damaged Delivery:

In an unlikely event of damages during delivery, artwork having quailty issue or wrong artwork delivered to you, it is eligible for free replacement. Customer shall inform Arte'Venue about the damaged artwork, quality issue or wrong artwork within 48 hours of delivery along the photos of artwork showing the issue as a proof. Arte'Venue shall not be liable to replace the artwork or refund the money if Arte'Venue is informed after completion of 48 hours from time of delivery.

Returns:

If you are not satisfied, we are happy to accept the return within 5 days of delivery for full refund. Please note that, Arte'Venue will arrange for the pick up of the artwork and customer will bear the return shipping charges. Arte'Venue will deduct the return delivery charges before making the refund. To be eligible for refund, the returned artwork Arte'Venue receives should be in new condition, exactly as it was delivered to you.

Please keep the artwork in condition it was received, and in original packaging. We will arrange for the pick up. Upon receipt of the returned Arte'Venue will ascertain the condition of the artwork prior to issuing refund/replacement. The artwork will not be eligible for refund in case further damages are found.

Please refer to section 5 of terms of use for detailed procedures and policies.
